Lemonmade Rules & Best Practices

♣Objective:
 The goal of Blackjack is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand without exceeding 21.
♦Values:
Number cards (2-10): Face Value
Face cards (Jack, Queen, King): 10 points
Ace: Either 1 or 11 points (whichever benefits the hand)
 ♠Starting the Game:
Each player is dealt two cards face up. The dealer gets two cards, one face up (upcard) and one face down (hole card).
Player's Turn: Players decide how to proceed based on their hand and the dealer's upcard.
 Player Actions:
Hit: Take another card to increase the hand value.
Stand: Keep your current hand.
Double Down: Double your bet, take one more card, and then stand.
Split:If you have two cards of the same value, split them into two hands (requires another bet).
 Dealer's Turn: The dealer must hit until their hand is 17 or higher. Some casinos require the dealer to hit on a soft 17 (a hand with Ace valued as 11). Here at the Lemonmade Blackjack Lounge we hit our soft 17s.
